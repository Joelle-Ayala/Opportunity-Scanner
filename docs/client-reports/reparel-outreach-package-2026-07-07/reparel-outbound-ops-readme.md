# Reparel Outbound Operations Package

Prepared: July 7, 2026
Source: Opportunity Scanner Reparel package

## Purpose

Use this package to review Reparel's public-sector opportunity signals and convert the strongest ones into outbound operations.

This is not a grant list and not an automatic send list. The strongest motion is buyer/channel validation for rehab, physical therapy, orthotic, VA/HHS, and DME-adjacent supply routes.

## Files

- `reparel-opportunity-signal-report.html` - visual report for review.
- `reparel-contacts.csv` - contact paths and connector-derived email candidates.
- `reparel-ai-outreach-package.md` - outreach-ready context, drafts, follow-ups, and CRM notes.
- `reparel-ai-outreach-package.csv` - structured upload file for outreach/CRM workflows.
- `reparel-ai-outreach-package.json` - structured upload file for AI outreach agents.
- `reparel-outbound-review-queue-2026-07-07.csv` - review queue for outbound operations.

## Recommended Operating Order

1. Review the visual report.
2. Use the review queue to decide which targets are approved, held, or need research.
3. Verify all Snov-generated email candidates before sending.
4. Resolve role-path contacts for First Nations Distribution, National Environmental, KLM Laboratories, and VA/Apex before outreach.
5. Prepare a one-page Reparel public-sector product note before outreach:
   - recovery sleeve
   - post-op support
   - brace/boot undersleeve
   - pain/swelling support
   - PT/rehab supply
   - orthotic-adjacent and DME-adjacent positioning
   - reimbursement/PDAC positioning where appropriate
6. Import the AI outreach CSV or JSON into the outbound workflow after review.
7. Log every send, reply, request for materials, and next step in CRM.

## Priority Read

Start with Performance Health because it has the clearest distributor/category fit and existing Snov candidates.

Then research First Nations Distribution, National Environmental, and KLM Laboratories because those are current or near-current HHS rehab/orthotics lanes.

Use Apex/VA and Footmaxx as market-map/channel evidence unless a stronger current buying office or named owner is found.

## Guardrails

- Do not send to unverified contacts.
- Do not treat USAspending awards as open solicitations.
- Do not pitch CGS, Empower AI, or Softrams as near-term buyers; they are DMEPOS/reimbursement infrastructure signals.
- Do not overclaim clinical outcomes.
- Do not invent emails or contacts.
- Keep Reparel separate from Jammcard and SchoolGig outreach.
