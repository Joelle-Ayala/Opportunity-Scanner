# Snov Campaign Sequence Drafts

Date: July 9, 2026

These are draft sequences for Snov once contact validation and suppression checks are in place. Do not enroll unverified contacts.

## Shared Rules

- Keep emails short.
- Link to the relevant industry sample report.
- Offer a free mini-scan as the CTA.
- Do not claim we already found opportunities for their company unless a company-specific scan has been run.
- Do not promise grants, contracts, or revenue.
- If a mini-scan has been run, include only 2-3 sourced signals in the free version and position the full report as the action layer.
