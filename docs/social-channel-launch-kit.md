## Primary Tagline

Turn your company website into public-sector revenue opportunities.

## Short Description

Opportunity Scanner helps businesses find public-sector funding, procurement, funded buyer, policy, workforce, reimbursement, and money-flow opportunities from their company website.

## Long Description

Opportunity Scanner turns messy public-sector data into business development actions. The product scans a company website, finds relevant public-source opportunity signals, and translates them into target organizations, revenue motions, contact paths, next best actions, outreach angles, and workflow-ready rows.

It is built for founders, revenue teams, partnerships leads, agencies, and operators who want to test public-sector revenue without starting with a full government contracting function.
