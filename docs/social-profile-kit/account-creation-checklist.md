# Social Account Creation Checklist

## Before Creating Accounts

- Activate a business-controlled inbox, preferably `hello@opportunityscanner.ai`.
- Use a password manager and a unique password for every platform.
- Turn on two-factor authentication immediately after each account is created.
- Store recovery codes in the password manager.
- Use Joelle as the initial owner and add a second trusted administrator where supported.
- Do not use a contractor's personal email as the only owner.
- Record the final handle, account URL, owner email, recovery method, and creation date.

## Recommended Creation Order

1. LinkedIn Company Page.
2. YouTube channel.
3. Instagram professional account and connected Threads profile.
4. X account.
5. TikTok Business Account.
6. Facebook Page, connected to the same Meta Business Portfolio as Instagram.
7. Substack publication.
8. Medium publication.
9. Optional Bluesky handle reservation.

## Per-Account Completion Check

- Preferred handle attempted before using a fallback.
- Profile name and capitalization match `Opportunity Scanner`.
- Correct avatar and platform-specific banner uploaded.
- Bio and long description match the creation kit.
- Website uses the platform-specific tracking link.
- Public contact email works and is monitored.
- Category and account type are correct.
- Two-factor authentication and recovery codes are complete.
- A backup administrator is added where supported.
- Introductory post is published and pinned where supported.
- Live account URL is recorded in the project launch tracker.

## Items That Require The Owner

- Email, phone, CAPTCHA, identity, and business verification.
- Choosing or confirming the public business location.
- Accepting platform terms.
- Selecting the final handle when the preferred handle is unavailable.
- Adding payment details for advertising or premium features; these are not required for organic launch.

Do not purchase verification, Premium Company Page access, advertising, or creator subscriptions during setup unless the founder approves the spend.
