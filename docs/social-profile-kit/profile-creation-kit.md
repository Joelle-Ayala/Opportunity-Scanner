### Short Description

Source-backed public-sector revenue intelligence from your company website.

### Standard Company Description

Opportunity Scanner helps businesses discover and qualify public-sector revenue opportunities from their company website. It searches official public sources for procurement, grants, funded buyers, recipients, primes, policy, workforce, reimbursement, and money-flow signals, then translates relevant records into buyer or partner targets, revenue motions, contact paths, next best actions, CRM-ready notes, and outreach angles.

Results remain connected to source evidence so teams can verify status and fit before pursuing them. Opportunity Scanner is built for founders, revenue leaders, partnerships teams, agencies, and operators exploring public-sector growth without starting with a full government-contracting team.

### Specialties

Public-sector revenue intelligence; public-sector opportunity discovery; government spending data; funded buyer intelligence; procurement intelligence; grant intelligence; business development; sales intelligence; partner discovery; contact paths; revenue motions; public-sector workflow.
