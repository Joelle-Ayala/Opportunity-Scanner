## Basic Citations / Source Coverage

Priority public sources to cite on site:

- SAM.gov: contract opportunities and procurement notice language.
- Grants.gov: grant-making agencies and federal grant opportunity context.
- USAspending.gov: federal award and spending records.
- Federal Register: policy and regulatory signals.
- Regulations.gov: public comment and regulatory signal context.
- BLS: workforce and labor-market context.
- Census: market, geography, and business context.
- CMS: healthcare and reimbursement-related signals.
- DOL: workforce funding, jobs, and employment programs.
